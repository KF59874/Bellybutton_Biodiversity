Project Overview

This website is designed to visualize research results from a study on biodiversity of bacteria found in belly buttons. The data is interactive, and shows the result of participants of the study. Choose the results of a participant by clicking on the dropdown menu for 'Test Subject ID No:' and choose an appropriate ID.

The objecive of this research is to find suitable bacteria out of which synthetic beef can be produced.

On this website, the data is visualized in three parts:

Bar chart on the 'Top 10 Bacteria Cultures Found' in each participant's belly button 'Belly Button Washing Frequency' per participant 'Bacteria Cultures per Sample'

If you would like to find out more about each category, simply hover over the bar chart or cultures to find out the name of the most prominent bacteria.
